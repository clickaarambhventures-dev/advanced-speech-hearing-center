Audiological Diagnostics

Hearing Tests We Provide

Accurate diagnosis is the foundation of good hearing care. We use internationally recognised tests for every age.

OAE
Newborn & Infant Screening

Otoacoustic Emissions (OAE)

A painless, quick test measuring the response of hair cells inside the cochlea. Ideal for newborn hearing screening. Results available in minutes — no active response required from the patient.

✅ Ideal for: Newborns & infants under 6 months
BERA
Objective Brain-Stem Test

BERA / ABR Test

Measures brainwave activity in response to sound without any active participation — perfect for young children or those with developmental delays. Safe and completely non-invasive.

✅ Ideal for: Infants, children, non-cooperative patients
PTA
Standard Hearing Evaluation

Pure Tone Audiometry (PTA)

The gold-standard hearing test. You listen to tones at different pitches via headphones; your responses plot an audiogram showing the exact nature and degree of hearing loss.

✅ Ideal for: Adults & children above 4 years
IMM
Middle Ear Assessment

Immittance / Tympanometry

Assesses eardrum health and middle ear movement. Diagnoses fluid in the ear (glue ear), ear infections & Eustachian tube dysfunction — common causes of hearing loss in children.

✅ Ideal for: All ages with repeated ear infections
Hearing Aid Services

Premium Hearing Aids in Begusarai

Free trial before you commit. Our audiologist matches the right device to your audiogram, lifestyle & budget.

BTE

Behind-the-Ear

Powerful & durable. Best for severe to profound hearing loss. Suitable for all ages including children.

RIC

Receiver-in-Canal

Slim, discreet, natural sound. Ideal for mild to moderately-severe hearing loss.

ITE

In-the-Ear

Custom-moulded. Easy to handle, good for mild-to-moderate loss.

CIC

Completely-in-Canal

Nearly invisible. Best for mild to moderate loss in active adults.
